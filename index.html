Capabilities

Everything You Need
In One Platform

From receptor upload to ADMET prediction — DockEase is the only platform where the entire structure-based drug design workflow lives in a single browser tab.

Multi-Format Molecular Input

Upload PDB, PDBQT, SDF, MOL2. Enter SMILES with live 2D preview. Import from RCSB PDB or PubChem by ID. Automatic 3D conformer generation for all inputs.

4 Docking Engines

AutoDock Vina (CPU, reliable), AutoDock-GPU (10–50× faster), Uni-Dock (GPU batch for tens of thousands of compounds), and Smina (custom scoring). Choose the right tool for each campaign.

Real-Time Progress Monitoring

Live job updates via WebSocket — not email, not polling. Watch your virtual screen progress compound by compound with an ETA estimate. Automatic reconnection on network interruption.

Interactive 3D Results Viewer

Click any docked pose to view it in the integrated NGL Viewer — right in the browser. Hydrogen bonds, hydrophobic contacts, and pi-stacking interactions are highlighted automatically.

Smart Binding Site Detection

P2Rank computational pocket detection automatically finds and ranks binding sites on your receptor. Or define a custom box manually in the 3D viewer. Residue annotations included.

ADMET Prediction

One-click drug-likeness and safety profiling for any hit. Absorption, distribution, metabolism, excretion, and toxicity properties including hERG, AMES, BBB penetration, CYP inhibition, and Lipinski compliance.

Industry Firsts

Two Features Nobody
Else Has

Score Calibration and Fragment Growing are unique to DockEase. They transform virtual screening from a hit-finding exercise into a complete lead optimisation workflow.

Industry First

Score Calibration

Raw docking scores are target-dependent. A −8 kcal/mol hit on EGFR means something completely different than −8 kcal/mol on a GPCR. Score Calibration uses your known actives and inactives to compute a statistically rigorous, target-specific hit threshold — complete with ROC curve and AUC analysis.

  • Upload known actives and inactives (DUD-E decoys supported)
  • Automatic ROC curve and AUC computation
  • Youden's J optimal threshold — maximises sensitivity + specificity
  • Apply threshold to auto-flag hits in any screening result
Industry First

Fragment Growing

Turn a docked lead into a structure-activity relationship in one click. Pick any attachment atom on your best-scoring pose, and DockEase enumerates 24 medicinal chemistry fragments, docks all variants, and ranks them by ΔΔG improvement. Weeks of SAR exploration, automated in minutes.

  • 24 standard medicinal chemistry R-groups enumerated automatically
  • All variants prepared and docked in a single batch job
  • Results ranked by ΔΔG relative to parent compound
  • Compare parent vs. variant poses in the 3D viewer side-by-side
Workflow

Four Steps to
Your First Hit

DockEase handles all the molecular preparation complexity in the background. You focus on the science.

1

Upload Your Receptor

Upload a PDB file or fetch directly from RCSB PDB by entering the 4-character code. DockEase auto-repairs missing residues, adds hydrogens, and prepares the PDBQT.

2

Add Ligands

Upload an SDF library, paste SMILES strings, or import from PubChem. From single compound to 10,000-compound virtual libraries — all auto-prepared with RDKit and Meeko.

3

Configure and Launch

Select a binding site (auto-detected or manually defined). Choose your docking engine. Set exhaustiveness. Click Launch. Watch real-time progress in the browser.

4

Analyse and Discover

Sort and filter ranked poses in the interactive table. Visualise binding modes in 3D. Run ADMET predictions. Grow fragments. Export hits as CSV or SDF.
